Meet Your Hairstylist

Talynn Garduno

Ever since attending cosmotology school over 10 years ago I have been extremely passionate about the art of hair color.  At Paul Micthell's Flagship School in Costa Mesa, CA, I became a protégé to some of the world's leading hair color artists Linda Tan, Heather Kaanoi, Robert Cromeans, and Angus Mitchell.  I also received a "Color Honors" award at School, and attended multiple workshops and events related to highlights, color, balayage, extensions, and more.

With thousands of hours focusing almost exclusively on highlights, balayage, and extensions, I can assure you that you are in safe hands, and you will leave my chair feeling happy, confident, relaxed, and free knowing your hair is exactly how you pictured  it.

I take pride in my attention to detail, and I use the finest luxury products in the industry.  I include a 5 minute relaxing scalp massage with every service and my clients love it!  I love what I do so much that I offer my famous "Love It or It's Free Gaurantee" to everyone that sits in my chair.

Pricing

All Of My Color Service Prices Are "Starter Prices" and Subject To Change Depending On Length, Thickness, & Density Of The Hair.  I Will Always Let You Know Beforehand What The Pricing Will Be.

Full Highlight

$185

Isolating select strands in the hair and treating them with a haircolor or lightener to make them lighter than their base/natural color. Highlights can add dimension by contrasting with the rest of the hair and are created with foils, a cap or special combs or brushes used for “painting on” the color.  Full Highlights includes foiling throughout the entire head.

Balayage

$195

Free-handing or sweeping hair color, lightener or toner downwards in soft strokes directly on the surface of the desired section. This method is used to create dimension with a natural, softer look.

Partial Highlight

$165

Strands of color placed around the face for a brightening or framing effect.

Most of your natural color will peek through from the back and around the nape of the neck.
