PRICING

All Of Our Color Service Prices Are "Starting At" & Subject To Change & Depend On Length, Thickness, & Density Of The Hair.

Full Highlight

$185

Isolating select strands in the hair and treating them with a haircolor or lightener to make them lighter than their base/natural color. Highlights can add dimension by contrasting with the rest of the hair and are created with foils, a cap or special combs or brushes used for “painting on” the color.  Full Highlights includes foiling throughout the entire head.

Balayage

$195

Free-handing or sweeping hair color, lightener or toner downwards in soft strokes directly on the surface of the desired section. This method is used to create dimension with a natural, softer look.

Partial Highlight

$165

Strands of color placed around the face for a brightening or framing effect.

Most of your natural color will peek through from the back and around the nape of the neck.
