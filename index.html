Meet Your Hairstylist

Talynn Garduno

Ever since attending cosmotology school over 10 years ago I have been extremely passionate about the art of hair color.  At Paul Micthell's Flagship School in Costa Mesa, CA, I became a protégé to some of the world's leading hair color artist's Heather Kaanoi, Angus Mitchell,  Stephanie Kocielski, and Robert Cromeans. Also, I received the "Color Honors" award there, and attended multiple workshops and events related to highlights, color, balayage, extensions, and more.

With thousands of hours focusing almost exclusively on highlights, balayage, and extensions, I can assure you that you are in safe hands with me!  You will leave my chair feeling happy, confident, relaxed, and free knowing your hair is exactly how you pictured  it.

My mission as a hairstylist is to provide a luxury hair service that exceeds my client's expectations everytime.  To help fufill my mission, I only use the finest luxury hair products in the industry on my client's hair.   I really want you to feel totally relaxed and at ease from the moment you arrive, so in addition to including a 5 minute head and neck massage with every service, I also include my "Zero-Risk" Love it or It's Free" Guarantee to all of my clients.

Pricing

All Of My Color Service Prices Are "Starter Prices" and Subject To Change Depending On Length, Thickness, & Density Of The Hair.  I Will Always Let You Know Beforehand What The Pricing Will Be.

Full Highlight

$185

Isolating select strands in the hair and treating them with a haircolor or lightener to make them lighter than their base/natural color. Highlights can add dimension by contrasting with the rest of the hair and are created with foils, a cap or special combs or brushes used for “painting on” the color.  Full Highlights includes foiling throughout the entire head.

Balayage

$200

Free-handing or sweeping hair color, lightener or toner downwards in soft strokes directly on the surface of the desired section. This method is used to create dimension with a natural, softer look.

Partial Highlight

$165

Strands of color placed around the face for a brightening or framing effect.

Most of your natural color will peek through from the back and around the nape of the neck.
