WHY Blonde?

Because Blonde Is "HARD"

"Blonde Fresno" was created out of the need for a hair experience created just for women with blonde hair or want blonde hair.  We know how one little mistake can turn into a disaster.  We specialize in fixing what other stylist's wreck, and we guarantee results.  Want the perfect balayage blonde highlights or extensions?  Look no further, "Blonde Fresno" is here.

A SALON WITHIN A SALON

Located inside Urban Chic Salon on Alluvial & Fresno Sreet In Fresno, "Blonde Fresno" is a special hair service owned & operated by Talynn Garduno, Fresno's Top Blonde Hair Expert.  With over 10 years of working almost exclusively with blonde hair, Talynn is the best of the best.
