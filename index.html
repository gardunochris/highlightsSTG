Meet Your Hairstylist

Talynn Garduno

Talynn Garduno is Fresno’s premiere hairstylist. She is located at Urban Chic Salon on Fresno St & Alluvial Avenue in Fresno, California. Talynn operates by appointment only, and offers a full range of services from highlights, haircuts, blowouts, and more. Talynn is so confident in her abilities that she offers a 100% Love It or It’s Free Guarantee!

Talynn prides herself in her attention to detail, especially with highlights. In fact, in her own words, “Fixing Cheetah hair is my mission.” Talynn promises you will love her attention to detail, and she will give you the hairstyle YOU want and love. “Too many women leave a hair salon feeling like they didn’t get what they wanted.” In fact, Talynn is so confident you will love her work, she is the ONLY hairstylist in town that offers her famous 100% Love It or It’s Free Guarantee!

Located At Urban Chic Salon In Fresno

Located inside Urban Chic Salon on Alluvial & Fresno Sreet In Fresno, "Highlights Fresno" is a special hair service owned & operated by Talynn Garduno, Fresno's Top Highlights & Blonde Expert.  With over 10 years of working almost exclusively with blonde hair, Talynn is the best of the best.
